# Business Context — Goolets

## Business Overview

Goolets je premium yacht charter agencija iz Slovenije. 20+ let izkušenj, 2,800+ charterjev, 30,000+ gostov. "Accessible luxury" — superyachti za skupine (do 36 gostov), €18K-€180K/teden. Glavni destinaciji: Hrvaška in Turčija.

## Business Goals

Primary KPI: CPQL (Cost Per Quality Lead)
Mode: Balanced

## Performance Targets

Target CPQL: €96 (SCALE zone upper limit)
Target Monthly QL: 200+
Target Channel Split: 55% FB / 45% Google

### CPQL Zone Framework

| Zona | CPQL Range | Akcija |
|------|-----------|--------|
| SCALE | < €96 | Povečaj budget 15-20% tedensko |
| MAINTAIN | €96 - €150 | Ohrani budget, optimiziraj kreative |
| OPTIMIZE | €150 - €240 | Zmanjšaj budget, testiraj nove pristope |
| CUT | > €240 | Pausaj ali drastično spremeni |

### Lead Quality Definitions

- **QL (Quality Lead):** Streak AI Score ≥ 50
- **EX (Excellent Lead):** Streak AI Score ≥ 70

### Current vs Target (Feb 2026)

- CPQL: Current €109 → Target €96 (Gap -14%)
- QL/Month: Current 235 → Target 200+ (on target)
- Channel Split: Current 67% FB / 33% Google → Target 55% / 45%
